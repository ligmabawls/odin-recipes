Taco Salad

Description

A yummy salad consisting of Doritos, your choice of protein, cheese, and delicious veggies!
For taco lovers who want to try soemthing new, give this recipe a try!

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Choice of protein: any kind of meat or beans
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Doritos, of course!

Steps

  1. Cook your choice of protein until done.
  2. Add Doritos to bowl.
  3. Add protein on top of Doritos.
  4. Add toppings to bowl, and enjoy!
